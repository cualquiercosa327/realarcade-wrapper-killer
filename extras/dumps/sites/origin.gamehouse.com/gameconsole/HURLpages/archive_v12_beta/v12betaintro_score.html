RealArcade v1.2 Beta 2

Preview the Next Version of RealArcade!

 The RealArcade Team has been hard at work on a new version of RealArcade, and invites you to try the Beta 2 version.

The new RealArcade features:

  • Faster installation
  • More reliable game downloads
  • Better game requirements checking
  • And of course, we fixed some bugs too!

So who should try the RealArcade v1.2 Beta 2? We're looking for help from savvy RealArcade customers to try out the nearly-complete new version and to:

  • Give us feedback on what they think (see the Feedback Form)
  • Let us know about any bugs they find (see the Bug Form)

If you love trying out the latest and greatest software and would like to share your opinions with us, we invite you to download and install the RealArcade v1.2 Beta by clicking this link:

Download the RealArcade v1.2 Beta (5.03 mb)

After trying Beta 2, please fill out Feedback Form
regardless of whether or not you encountered bugs

 
A few notes about RealArcade v1.2 Beta 2:
  • You can install the Beta 2 on top of an existing version of RealArcade without losing your RealArcade games (with two exceptions: please see Known Bugs below).
  • The Beta 2 period will run for one week. At the end of the Beta 2 period, you'll need to download and install the Final version of RealArcade v1.2 to replace your Beta.
  • For the duration of the Beta 2 period, the RealArcade v1.2 Beta will be supported by the RealArcade team directly, instead of by RealNetworks customer service.

Known bugs

  • RealArcade v1.2 Beta 2 has problems finding the full version of Diamond Mine and the demo & full versions of Tex Atomic's Big Bot Battles. This will be fixed in the final version.

Thanks for your help!
