HotSync Instructions

To copy this game from RealOne Arcade to your handheld, you will need to perform a HotSync. First, you'll need to follow these steps from RealOne Arcade:

1. Click the "HotSync Game to Palm" button in RealOne Arcade.

2. This dialog will appear, and you should see the game in the list.

3. Click the "Done" button

Now you're ready to do the HotSync from your handheld. If your handheld is connected to your computer via a dock, simply press the HotSync button on the dock. Otherwise, follow these steps on your handheld:

1. Tap the Home icon on your handheld.

2. Tap the HotSync icon on the screen.

3. Tap the HotSync icon on the center of the screen.

After a few seconds, the HotSync will be complete. Tap the Home icon again, and you will see the game listed among the applications installed on your handheld.
