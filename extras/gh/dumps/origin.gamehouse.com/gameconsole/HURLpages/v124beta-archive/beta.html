RealOne Arcade v1.24 Beta

  

 
Preview RealOne Arcade v1.24!
RealOne Arcade v1.24 features a smoother installation process, includes several bug fixes, and will be the first version of RealOne Arcade to be available in languages other than English.

 
What is a Beta?
The RealOne Arcade v1.24 Beta is a special version of RealArcade that is near completion. RealNetworks is offering the Beta to solicit feedback. The Beta Testing process is an important part of creating new products at RealNetworks and helps to ensure that the final product is of the highest possible quality!

 
Who Should Try the RealOne Arcade v1.24 Beta?
If you're Windows-savvy, love to try the latest software, and are willing to give us feedback and let us know if you encounter bugs - we invite you to download the Beta today!

 
How Do I Report a Bug or Give Feedback on the RealOne Arcade v1.24 Beta?
When you encounter a bug, please take note of how you encountered it and let us know about it. For feedback, please fill out the product feedback form.
