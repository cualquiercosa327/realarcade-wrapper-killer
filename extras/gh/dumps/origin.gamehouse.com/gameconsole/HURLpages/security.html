RealOne Arcade AutoUpdate and Your Privacy

AutoUpdate is an easy, reliable way to make sure you always have the latest and greatest software.

  1. Provides a secure and reliable conduit for providing new software plug-ins to your desktop.

  2. Gives you an easy way to keep your RealNetworks' products up-to-date with the most current versions of all of its components.

  3. AutoUpdate provides all of these benefits seamlessly --without you having to go through lengthy downloads or reinstallations.

Secure and Safe Delivery Of Software

Given our large and growing customer base, AutoUpdate was designed to safely manage software downloads to RealOne Arcade users.

RealNetworks makes use of "digital certificates" that use Digital Signature Algorithms (DSA) adapted from the Digital Signature Standard, a U.S Government designed & approved public/private key algorithm to sign and verify digital documents. Each update is 'signed' with a digital certificate. These signatures utilize a 1024-bit structure for maximum security and can only be verified by an algorithm (a mathematical formula) within the product you are using.

After a component is downloaded from RealNetworks' servers to your hard drive, but before it is decompressed or installed, the digital signature is checked to verify the source and that the file hasn't been changed or tampered with since it was signed.

If the authentication process is successful, the new component will be decompressed and installed. If it fails, the file will be deleted from your hard drive.

AutoUpdate: When does it occur?

The AutoUpdate process was designed to intelligently detect and download software updates to RealOne Arcade.  RealOne Arcade will automatically check for updates once every 10 days when you launch the application.  If RealOne Arcade detects that an update is available, it will prompt you to update those components.  If you accept the upgrade, the AutoUpdate process will begin.  If you do not accept the upgrade, RealOne Arcade will not remind you again for 10 days.

The AutoUpdate process can also be initiated manually. Just select the RealOne Arcade "?" button and select Check for RealOne Arcade Update.

Some of your games from RealNetworks can also be AutoUpdated.  Check the Support tab of the Game Page for each of your games.  If you see Check for Updates, you can click on that at any time to determine if there is a patch available for your game.  If you see Check for Patches, that game does NOT support AutoUpdate, but you can click on that link to go to a patch site where the latest game patch may reside.  If you do not see Check for Updates or Check for Patches on the Support tab of the Game Page, the game does not have update or patch information for it.

Seamless Delivery Of Product Updates

After an initial installation, you can get continuous software updates at the click of a button. AutoUpdate works "behind the scenes" first taking an inventory of your current RealOne Arcade and then offering to update only those portions that need to be. This approach greatly simplifies and shortens the download process.

AutoUpdate and Your Privacy

What Information is Sent to RealNetworks?
  • Product Name (e.g. RealOne Arcade)
  • Product Version
  • Product Type (Basic only)
  • Distribution Code - to offer you software customized for the source of your player or jukebox
  • Player Language ID
  • Operating System
  • Country ID
  • Components Installed and component versions - list of the software components that you already have so that we do not present the same software to you again
  • Days since last AutoUpdate check

How This Information is Used

The anonymous information sent during AutoUpdate is required for RealNetworks to determine whether there is new, free software available for you to download.

RealNetworks may use some of this anonymous information for aggregate statistical analysis to better understand how our products are being used.

Privacy Implications

No private information is sent with this data.
