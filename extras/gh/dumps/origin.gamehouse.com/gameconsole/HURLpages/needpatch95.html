RealOne Arcade and Windows 95

To run on Microsoft Windows 95, RealOne Arcade requires the DCOM 95 update.

The DCOM 95 update is included in Internet Explorer 5.0 and higher, and is also available as a separate download.

  • To download the latest version of Internet Explorer from Microsoft, click here
  • To download the DCOM 95 patch from Microsoft, click here (approximately 1.2 MB).
